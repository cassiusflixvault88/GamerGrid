## Application Details:

### 1. Business Use Case:
FlixVault is a streaming platform aggregating free-to-watch content from multiple sources including public domain films, Archive.org, and partner platforms. We want to integrate Tubi's free movie catalog to provide our users with access to modern, high-quality films (2000s-2024) alongside classic content.

### 2. Integration Goals:
- Display Tubi's free movie catalog within FlixVault
- Provide proper attribution and branding for Tubi content
- Drive traffic to Tubi's platform when users click "Watch Now"
- Estimated user base: Growing community platform (currently in beta)

### 3. Technical Requirements:
- Access to Tubi Partner API for movie metadata (titles, posters, descriptions, genres)
- Ability to fetch streaming URLs or deep links to Tubi platform
- Real-time availability checking

### 4. Revenue Model:
- Free platform with potential premium features in future
- Will maintain Tubi branding and attribution
- Will drive user engagement to Tubi platform
